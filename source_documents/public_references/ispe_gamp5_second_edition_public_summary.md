# ISPE GAMP 5 second edition public summary

The public ISPE page describes GAMP 5 as a good-practice framework for computerized systems that are effective, high quality, fit for intended use, and compliant with applicable regulations. The second edition highlights supplier involvement, iterative and incremental development methods, software tools and automation, critical thinking by knowledgeable SMEs, data integrity links, cloud computing, and AI/ML appendices.

## Audit-planning relevance

- ACME Pharma's AI lifecycle should demonstrate fit-for-intended-use thinking rather than generic validation paperwork.
- Supplier knowledge and supplier documentation may be useful, but the regulated company keeps accountability for intended use and compliance.
- Audit should test whether AI/ML, cloud, supplier, data integrity, and change-control topics are connected in one lifecycle process.
