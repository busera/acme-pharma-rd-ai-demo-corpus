## Audit-planning relevance

The Machine Learning Lens organizes practices across the ML lifecycle and the Well-Architected pillars. For this synthetic R&D AI engagement, it supports questions about:

- accountable ML roles and lifecycle ownership;
- model, data, configuration, and deployment lineage;
- privacy, permissions, licensing, least privilege, and secure ML environments;
- observability, drift, performance degradation, retraining, and human monitoring;
- repeatable deployment and change controls.
