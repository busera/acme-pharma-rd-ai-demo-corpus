# HMA-EMA multi-annual AI workplan 2023-2028

The HMA-EMA workplan frames AI in medicines regulation around guidance and policy, tools and technologies, collaboration and change management, and experimentation. It emphasizes responsible use, data protection, training, stakeholder engagement, and monitored roll-out of AI tools.

## Audit-planning relevance

- AI governance in life sciences should include policy, tool controls, training, collaboration, and experimentation guardrails.
- The workplan supports treating AI adoption as an organizational change and governance topic, not only a technology implementation.
- Audit can use this source to justify interviews with policy owners, tool owners, training leads, data protection, and experimentation sponsors.
