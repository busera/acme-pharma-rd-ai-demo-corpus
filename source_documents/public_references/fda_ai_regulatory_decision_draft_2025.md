# FDA Draft Guidance: AI Supporting Regulatory Decision-Making

## Authority and status

- **Issuer:** US Food and Drug Administration
- **Date:** January 2025
- **Status:** Draft guidance; not for implementation; non-binding recommendations
- **Official source:** <https://www.fda.gov/media/184830/download>

## Audit-planning relevance

The draft proposes a risk-based credibility assessment framework for AI models used to produce information or data supporting regulatory decision-making for drug and biological products. Its seven steps are:

1. define the question of interest;
2. define the AI model context of use;
3. assess model risk;
4. plan how credibility will be established for that context;
5. execute the plan;
6. document results and deviations;
7. determine adequacy for the context of use.

The draft also flags lifecycle maintenance where performance can change over time or across deployment environments.
